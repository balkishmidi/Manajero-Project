SAFe Methodology

A Comprehensive Tutorial

Step-by-Step Guide to SAFe

Step 1: Learn About SAFe

Understand the basics of SAFe and its core principles.

  • Read about SAFe: Familiarize yourself with the SAFe framework by reading key literature and resources available on the SAFe website.
  • Get Certified: Consider SAFe training and certification for deeper understanding and credibility.

Step 2: Train Lean-Agile Change Agents

Prepare a group of internal change agents who will lead the transformation.

  • Identify Change Agents: Select individuals who will become SAFe Program Consultants (SPCs).
  • Enroll in Training: Send these individuals to SPC training courses to gain the necessary knowledge and skills.

Step 3: Train Executives, Managers, and Leaders

Ensure leadership understands and supports the SAFe transformation.

  • Conduct Workshops: Organize workshops and training sessions for executives and managers to learn about SAFe and their role in the transformation.
  • Align Leadership: Ensure leadership is aligned with SAFe principles and committed to supporting the implementation.

Step 4: Identify Value Streams and Agile Release Trains (ARTs)

Organize the work around value delivery.

  • Map Value Streams: Identify the value streams in your organization that deliver value to customers.
  • Define ARTs: Group teams into Agile Release Trains (ARTs) that will deliver value incrementally.

Step 5: Create Implementation Plan

Develop a detailed plan for implementing SAFe.

  • Plan Milestones: Set clear milestones and timelines for the SAFe implementation.
  • Identify Metrics: Determine key metrics to track progress and success.

Step 6: Prepare for ART Launch

Get ready to launch the first Agile Release Train.

  • Train Teams: Provide SAFe training to all teams that will be part of the ART.
  • Set Up Infrastructure: Ensure that all necessary tools and infrastructure are in place to support SAFe practices.

Step 7: Train Teams and Launch ART

Begin the journey of delivering value through the ART.

  • Conduct PI Planning: Hold the first Program Increment (PI) Planning session to align all teams on objectives and deliverables.
  • Start Iterations: Begin executing iterations as planned during PI Planning.

Step 8: Coach ART Execution

Provide ongoing support and coaching to ensure successful execution.

  • Support Teams: Offer continuous coaching and support to teams to help them adhere to SAFe practices.
  • Monitor Progress: Regularly review progress and adjust plans as necessary.

Step 9: Launch More ARTs and Value Streams

Scale the SAFe implementation across the organization.

  • Repeat Steps 4-8: For each new ART and value stream, follow the same steps to ensure consistent implementation.
  • Coordinate ARTs: Synchronize multiple ARTs to ensure they work together effectively.

Step 10: Extend to the Portfolio

Implement SAFe at the portfolio level for strategic alignment.

  • Implement Lean Portfolio Management: Apply SAFe principles at the portfolio level to align strategy and execution.
  • Manage Epics: Use lean portfolio management to prioritize and manage large initiatives (Epics).

Step 11: Sustain and Improve

Continuously improve SAFe practices and sustain the transformation.

  • Conduct Inspect and Adapt Workshops: Regularly hold Inspect and Adapt workshops to identify improvements.
  • Foster a Culture of Continuous Improvement: Encourage a culture where continuous improvement is part of the organizational mindset.
